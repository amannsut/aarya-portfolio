Retail · 2025

The Discs

2025

Retail Store

New Delhi, India

2,400 sq ft

The Discs is a concept record store that reimagines the vinyl shopping experience as a cultural destination. Drawing inspiration from the circular geometry of vinyl records, the spatial design uses curved forms, acoustic ceiling treatments, and rich red tones to create an immersive, sensory environment. The layout encourages discovery — customers meander through curated listening zones before arriving at the central counter.

Residential · 2024

The Knit Cafe

2024

Residential

New Delhi, India

3,200 sq ft

The Knit Café reimagines the former Café Dori site at Dhan Mill Compound as a 225 m² warm, community-driven space that blends café culture with the tactile art of knitting. Designed as a flexible, multi-use environment, the project transforms the existing warehouse setting into a cozy yet open studio café where visitors can gather, work, create, and unwind.

Urban Streetscrapping · 2025

The Kerala Project

2025

Urban Streetscrapping

Kochi, India

8 x 40 meters

This streetscaping project reimagines an 8m x 40m stretch connecting the Spice Trade Centre to the Crafters Store in Jew Town, Kochi. Rooted in the area’s colonial architecture, spice heritage, and vibrant retail culture, the design enhances pedestrian experience while preserving the historic character of the street.
